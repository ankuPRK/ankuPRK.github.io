Biography

I’m a graduate student pursuing Masters in Computer Vision (MSCV) at CMU. My interests lie in Computer Vision, Image and Video processing and Machine Learning.

I was employed at Samsung R&D Institute Bangalore (SRIB) for 2 years. I joined Keyboard team in Advanced Technology Labs, where I worked on Keyboard Swipe generation and N-gram LM architecture for Indian languages. After winning SRIB C-Lab Challenge I got transferred to Creative Lab under CTO, where I led a 3-member team of ‘intrapreneurs’ for designing and building a food grain quality estimation app with On-Device DNN engine [blog]. During my Bachelors at IIT Kanpur I worked with Prof. Tanaya Guha on the problem of Online Face Clustering.

I am passionate about building Computer Vision solutions which solve real world problems, and impact people’s lives in a positive way.
